South Korea

This year, South Korea aims to fuse the traditional and modern cultures of Korea. We have selected pieces that were sensational breakthroughs in Korean culture and promoted Korean culture to the world. We want to introduce the audience to other subcultures in Korea that may not be as well known as the core K-pop idol group culture.
Each piece represents different aspects of Korean culture - traditional, K-hip-hop, and K-pop.

The first piece, ‘Hanryang’ fuses traditional Korean music with modern hiphop culture. This piece shows the transcending beauty of traditional Korean culture. Next is ‘All I wanna do’ by Jay Park, a Korean hiphop artist who puts in a lot of effort in promoting Korean hiphop culture to the world. The finale of our performance is Big Bang’s ‘Bang Bang Bang’, the most iconic K-pop boy group in K-pop history.

We aim to represent the spirit of '꾼(kkun)', the spirit of having true enjoyment and fun, through our joyful and upbeat pieces. As we unite the different subcultures through the spirit of 꾼, we tie in with the theme 'Reunion'.
