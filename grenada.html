Grenada

In this year's Culturama, Grenada aims to convey the message of ‘reunion’ by showcasing the fluorescent and free spirit characteristics of the one-of-a-kind festival called ‘carnival’ which fills up streets annually during the month of August.

As carnival is the most vital celebration in Grenada, we have incorporated dance styles of Caribbean origin such as Afrobeats, dance hall, and Soca to highlight the multi-influential background of Grenada brought in by larger neighbouring Caribbean countries. The core of Carnival comprises of music, costume and dance, which you will see reflected in our performance through a colourful display and popular upbeat songs that would be found playing during the Carnival celebration. As this is Grenadas first appearance to Culturama, we hope the audience is captivated by the burst of energy we present on stage as this encapsulates what Grenadian culture is all about.
