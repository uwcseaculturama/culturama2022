India B

The focus of our piece will be on representing Indian weddings and the many exuberant ceremonies displaying India’s rich and lively traditions and cultural practices. Indian weddings encompass an environment where extended families gather together to celebrate the joyous and auspicious moment of the union of two individuals. All these ceremonies allow families to reunite and immerse themselves in the happiness of building connections with their loved ones. Through our piece, we have chosen to represent the Haldi and Sangeet ceremonies. The Haldi Ceremony is conducted to cleanse and purify the body of the bride and groom before the wedding. The Sangeet Ceremony is conducted to rejoice and provides an opportunity for families to familiarise themselves with each other, and there is an atmosphere of enthusiasm and energy. The end of the two India dance pieces ties together to demonstrate the ‘Shaadi’. Through the intertwining of the various elements of our dance, from the choreography to the costumes, we aim to showcase a high-spirited reunion between relatives and friends.
